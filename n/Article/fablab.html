What is a FABLAB?

A FABLAB (Fabrication Laboratory) is a creative space equipped with digital manufacturing technologies that allow users to design, prototype, and fabricate various objects. It fosters an environment of innovation, where students, inventors, and startups can experiment, collaborate, and develop cutting-edge products. The CTU Danao FABLAB is part of a global network of similar facilities, each contributing to the democratization of technology.

Tools and Equipment

The CTU Danao FABLAB is outfitted with state-of-the-art equipment such as:

  • 3D Printers: These allow users to create three-dimensional objects from digital models, revolutionizing product design and prototyping.
  • Laser Cutters: Precision cutting tools that can work with materials like wood, plastic, and metal, perfect for intricate designs and manufacturing.
  • CNC Machines: Computer-controlled machining tools that can cut and shape solid materials into precise parts, widely used in engineering and manufacturing.
